About Tango Gardens

Where nature meets celebration

Welcome to Tango Gardens, a hidden gem in the heart of Ruiru, Kiambu County. Our venue is nestled in lush greenery, offering a peaceful escape from the bustling city while remaining easily accessible from Nairobi.

Founded with a vision to create a space where nature, hospitality, and celebration intertwine, Tango Gardens has become the premier destination for those seeking unforgettable experiences. Our spacious grounds provide the perfect backdrop for weddings, corporate events, intimate dinners, and joyous celebrations.

At Tango Gardens, we believe in creating moments that matter. From the carefully manicured gardens to the attentive service, every detail is designed to ensure your event is nothing short of extraordinary.

Directions from Nairobi

Take Thika Superhighway towards Ruiru. Exit at Ruiru Town and follow the signs to Tango Gardens. Approximately 30 minutes from Nairobi CBD.

Directions from Ruiru Town

From Ruiru Town Centre, take the Ruiru-Kimbo Road for 5km. Tango Gardens will be on your left, clearly marked with our signage.
